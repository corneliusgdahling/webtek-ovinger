P1 Project Requirments for Spa Thai

Eirik Kaldahl, Egil Uggerud, Edvard Sjøblom, Simen Johansen, Cornelius Dahling - 22 september 2015

Client

Our client is Spa Thai, located in Olav Tryggvasons Gate 50 in Trondheim. They deliver different treatments/massages and spa-related products.

Contact person: Suban Andestad (suban@spathai.no)
Rolle: Daglig leder

The site's purpose and goals

The site’s main purpose is to attract users to go to Spa Thai to receive treatment or purchase products. Therefore the site needs to highlight everything a user can expect at Spa Thai. The site also needs to have a professional look and feel because it reflects the business as a whole. It is also vital that the users of the site has very clear access to contact information because all transactions are handled in the shop at Spa Thai.

Their customer base consists of people from different age groups. So we have to consider that not all visitors possess the same skillset in regard to navigating a website.

Audience

The target audience will mainly consist of people who are looking for, or want to book, spa or massage treatment. Key information for these potential users are the different products/services and pricing. Spa Thai also emphasizes their treatment for sore/stiff muscles which will make the site relevant for people that are suffering such ailments.

Content of the Website and Organization

The site will contain all the necessary contact information, as well as the different treatments that are offered and what they cost. It will contain the pages:

  • Hjem

    Will contain a short introduction of their Spa, treatments and location. This will be a very important page and will give new visitors a first impression of Spa Thai. The goal is tempting the visitors to consider ordering a treatment.

  • Behandlinger

    Here the users will get thorough about the many different treatments Spa Thai has to offer. This section will also tell the users about the price, so that he doesn’t have to go back the “Prisliste”-page all the time. Also, pictures of the treatments will be there to give a short preview of what the users can expect.

  • Prisliste

    This section will show the prices of all the treatments, so the users can get a good overview of all the prices at once. This way it’s easier for the users to scope in on the treatments that’s within their price range. Even though we already have prices on each treatments in the “Behandlinger”-page, the client wanted to have an overview containing all the prices in one small area.

  • Produkter

    This section displays the different products they sell such as lotions, gift cards, oils and so forth.

  • English summary

    A short and concise summary of all the other sections in a one page HTML written in English. We might also make the site fully supported in english but this is not a requirement but rather a suggestion for expansion.

Specifications / Requirements

Specifications

  1. All pages shall have a similar look and feel

    On the existing website, all the different pages does not have the same colours. Thai Spa has requested that all sub-pages on the new site should have a similar look and feel.

  2. The website should be layed out in a logical and tidy order

    The existing website is very unattractive and too much information from different categories are shoved in your face. The client has asked us to organize the new site so that visitors more easily can find waht they are looking for and not be overwhelmed.

    They want the most important information such as products/services, contact information and prices to be in focus.

  3. Pictures needs to be larger

    For the new website they have requested that their pictures will be displayed in a larger format.

Non-functional requirments

  • The website shall be easy to use for vistiors in all age groups
  • The site shall have a summary page written in English
  • The site shall have a contact-form

Final location of the website

The website will be linked to the customers existing domain www.spathai.no
